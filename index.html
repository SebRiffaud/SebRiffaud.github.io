Sébastien Riffaud

I am a postdoctoral researcher at École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne
(Switzerland) in the CSQI chair under the supervision of Prof. Fabio Nobile.
My current research activity focuses on the developement of dynamical low-
rank approximations for accelerating data assimilation algorithms.

Research interests:

  • Numerical analysis, scientific computing, projection-based reduced-order models, dynamical low-rank approximations, inverse problems, data assimilation, computational fluid dynamics, hemodynamics
